1. DOM Heuristics (Content Script)

These buttons dynamically inject elements or alter page parameters to trigger content.js scanning. Once triggered, open the PhishGuard extension popup to see the threat listing or watch for warning overlays.

Injects a password input with autocomplete="off", a technique often used in phishing templates.
